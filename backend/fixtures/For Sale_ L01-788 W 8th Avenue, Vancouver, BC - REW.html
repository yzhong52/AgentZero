About this home

Spacious 3 bedroom reverse-layout townhome located in the heart of Fairview. The main living area showcases impressive city and mountain views and soaring ceilings. Enjoy cozy evenings by the gas fireplace, or relaxing on your private rooftop patio. Ideally located within walking distance to Broadway, Granville Island, & rapid transit. 2 parking stalls & 1 storage locker included. No dogs.
