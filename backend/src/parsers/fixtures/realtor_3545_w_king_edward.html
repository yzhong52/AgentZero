Listing Description

Charming 5 bed 2.5 bath located in the heart of Dunbar. Lord Byng & Lord Kitchner School catchment. Spanning 2,424 sqft, this partially renovated solid residence offers a blank canvas for your design ideas. The bright and spacious main floor features large living and dining areas with a 1 bed and powder room. Surrounded by windows the updated kitchen features newer appliances, centre island, plenty of cabinet space, and access to the back deck. Upstairs find access to balcony overlooking backyard, and 2 bedrooms including spacious principal. Basement features 2 additional bedrooms, laundry facilities and access to lower back deck. Home features newer paint, hardwood floors, updated bathrooms and light fixtures. Close to Dunbar shops, parks, and top schools. Open house Sat. Feb 28th 12-2. (32483752)
